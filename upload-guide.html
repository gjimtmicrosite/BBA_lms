📘 Submission Guidelines

All students enrolled in the BBA with AI Integration Program must upload project files, reports, screenshots, presentations, and reflection documents according to the submission structure shared by faculty.

✅ Important: Students must complete manual work first before using AI tools like ChatGPT, Canva AI, Copilot, or automation tools.

🗂️ File Naming Format

Project Recommended File Name
P1 — AI Business Research BBA_AI_P1_StudentName.pdf
P2 — Marketing Campaign BBA_AI_P2_StudentName.pptx
P3 — AI Startup Model BBA_AI_P3_StudentName.docx
P4 — AI Dashboard BBA_AI_P4_StudentName.pbix
💡 Tip: Always use your official student name and avoid spaces or special characters.

📁 Required Submission Items

Project Required Files
P1 Research report, screenshots, AI prompts, competitor analysis, presentation
P2 Campaign strategy, Canva creatives, social media mockups, AI content report
P3 Business model canvas, startup presentation, AI workflow screenshots, reflection report
P4 Dashboard file, Excel data, charts, AI insight report, final presentation

📤 How to Upload Your Work

Step 1 — Prepare Files

  • Check file naming format
  • Combine screenshots into PDF if required
  • Verify all links and files open correctly

Step 2 — Open Submission Form

  • Go to the Google Form shared by faculty
  • Select your project and section
  • Upload all required documents

Step 3 — Submit Carefully

  • Verify uploaded files before final submission
  • Use official Gmail ID
  • Keep screenshots of submission confirmation
⚠️ Warning: Late submissions may reduce project evaluation marks. Missing files may result in viva rejection.

🤖 AI Usage Guidelines

Allowed Not Allowed
