📁 BBA AI Integrated Shadow Projects

Each project combines business fundamentals with AI tools. Students complete manual analysis first and then use AI tools for enhancement, automation, dashboards, and insights.

P1 Retail Analysis P2 Social Media Campaign P3 Startup Case Study P4 Business Dashboard

🛍️ P1: Retail Store Market Analysis Reliance / DMart Weeks 1–3

Objective: Study a retail business model, customer segments, competitors, and local market opportunities.

📋 4-Phase Framework

🟠 Phase 1 — Business Understanding
  • Study Reliance Retail or DMart business model
  • Visit one nearby retail store physically
  • Observe pricing, product layout, offers, customer flow
  • Create handwritten observation notes
🟠 Phase 2 — Customer Analysis
  • Identify target customers and buying behaviour
  • Conduct survey with minimum 10 customers
  • Create SWOT analysis manually
🤖 Phase 3 — AI Business Insights
  • Use ChatGPT for competitor analysis
  • Use Google Trends for product demand research
  • Create AI-generated marketing suggestions
🤖 Phase 4 — Final Presentation
  • Create Canva AI presentation
  • Prepare 5-minute viva presentation
  • Submit report and reflection note

📊 Evaluation Rubric

Criteria Weight Focus
Business Understanding 30% Retail model and market clarity
Customer Analysis 30% Survey quality and insights
AI Usage 40% Prompt quality and AI-enhanced recommendations
📤 Submit P1

📱 P2: Social Media Marketing Campaign Instagram / YouTube Weeks 4–6

Objective: Create a social media campaign for a small business using AI tools and marketing strategies.

📋 4-Phase Framework

🟠 Phase 1 — Brand Study
  • Select a local business or startup
  • Study Instagram and YouTube presence
  • Identify strengths and weaknesses
🟠 Phase 2 — Content Planning
  • Create content calendar manually
  • Design customer persona
  • Write campaign goals and KPIs
🤖 Phase 3 — AI Content Creation
  • Use ChatGPT for captions and hashtags
  • Use Canva AI for posters and reels
  • Create AI-based engagement strategy
🤖 Phase 4 — Analytics & Report
  • Create engagement dashboard
  • Compare before vs after strategy
  • Prepare campaign presentation

📊 Evaluation Rubric

Criteria Weight Focus
Campaign Planning 30% Marketing strategy and planning
Content Quality 30% Visuals and communication
AI Enhancement 40% AI tools and analytics usage
📤 Submit P2

🚀 P3: Startup Case Study Indian Startup Ecosystem Weeks 7–9

Objective: Analyse a startup’s business model, growth strategy, funding, and future potential.

📋 4-Phase Framework

🟠 Phase 1 — Startup Research
  • Select one Indian startup
  • Study founders, product, customers, funding
  • Create company profile manually
🟠 Phase 2 — Business Model Analysis
  • Prepare Business Model Canvas
  • Analyse competitors and revenue streams
  • Estimate growth opportunities
🤖 Phase 3 — AI Strategic Analysis
  • Use ChatGPT for SWOT and market analysis
  • Generate AI business recommendations
  • Create future growth scenarios
🤖 Phase 4 — Investor Pitch
  • Create investor-style presentation
  • Present startup viability report
  • Submit AI reflection note

📊 Evaluation Rubric

Criteria Weight Focus
Startup Research 30% Accuracy and depth of analysis
Business Model 30% BMC and revenue analysis
AI Strategy 40% Strategic recommendations and insights
📤 Submit P3

📊 P4: AI Business Dashboard Excel AI / Power BI Weeks 10–12

Objective: Build a KPI dashboard using business data and AI-generated insights.

📋 4-Phase Framework

🟠 Phase 1 — KPI Planning
  • Select Sales, HR, Marketing, or Finance domain
  • Identify 4 KPIs manually
  • Sketch dashboard layout
🟠 Phase 2 — Data Preparation
  • Clean Excel dataset
  • Create pivot tables manually
  • Calculate KPI formulas
🤖 Phase 3 — AI Dashboard Creation
  • Use Excel AI / Copilot
  • Create Power BI visuals
  • Generate AI-based business insights
🤖 Phase 4 — Final Dashboard Submission
  • Create executive dashboard
  • Add AI-generated summary
  • Present final insights

📊 Evaluation Rubric

Criteria Weight Focus
KPI Design 30% SMART KPI selection
Data Accuracy 30% Correct calculations and cleaning
Dashboard Quality 40% Visuals, AI insights, interactivity
📤 Submit P4
