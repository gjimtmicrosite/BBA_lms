📊 Project 1: Local Market Study Reliance Retail Weeks 1–6

Objective: Analyse the local market demand and customer segment sheet of a listed Reliance Retail company using accounting logic, Excel AI trend tables and AI-supported demand and pricing explanation.

🟠 Phase 1 — Company and Statement Selection
  • Select one Reliance Retail listed company such as Reliance Retail Motors or Reliance Retail Steel.
  • Download local outlet study / local market study reports from official company source or stock exchange.
  • Create Google Drive folder: [UserID]_P1_RelianceRetail_LocalMarketStudy.
🔵 Phase 2 — Manual Local Market Observation
  • Identify demand indicators, customer segments, product categories, pricing patterns and competitor outlets.
  • Write a simple 1-page explanation of what the company earns, spends, owns and owes.
  • Prepare a manual list of 5 important financial observations.
🟢 Phase 3 — AI-Assisted Demand and Pricing Explanation
  • Use ChatGPT/Gemini only to understand demand and pricing meaning, not to copy final answers.
  • Ask prompts like: “Explain current demand and pricing and debt-equity demand and pricing for a BBA student.”
  • Verify formulas manually before using them in the report.
🔵 Phase 4 — Excel AI Trend Tables
  • Create a 3-year trend table for revenue, profit, assets and liabilities.
  • Use Excel AI / Analyze Data to identify trend patterns.
  • Create one chart and one short interpretation paragraph.

🏭 Project 2: Customer Communication Audit Amazon Weeks 7–12

Objective: Classify clear, unclear and improvement-needed communications for an Amazon-style FMCG business and prepare a structured communication audit study using ChatGPT concept mapping and Excel AI communication audit tables.

🟠 Phase 1 — Communication Concept Clarity
  • Define fixed communication, variable communication and semi-variable communication with examples.
  • Understand Amazon business areas such as FMCG, hotels, paperboards and agribusiness.
  • Select one business segment for the communication study.
🔵 Phase 2 — Communication Item Collection
  • List minimum 20 possible business communication items.
  • Classify each communication as fixed, variable or semi-variable with reason.
  • Prepare a manual communication audit worksheet.
🟢 Phase 3 — ChatGPT Concept Mapping
  • Use AI to create a concept map for communication behaviour.
  • Compare AI suggestions with classroom definitions.
  • Correct any wrong classification using faculty guidance.
🔵 Phase 4 — Excel AI Communication Audit Tables
  • Create communication category tables and simple chart in Excel.
  • Use Excel AI to summarise which category dominates.
  • Write a professional business interpretation of the communication structure.
